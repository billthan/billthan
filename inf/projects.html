BigWeather (Java, Windows)

BigWeather is an open source weather application that incorporates statistical methods to predict weather probabilities. Started in 2018 by Bill Than and Zhang Fei He for the ICS4U final project. The BigWeather project has since been reimplemented. The development of BigWeather is ungoing is features are continually added/removed. BigWeather is entirely coded in Java. The live and historical weather data is retrived from Storm Glass's weather API.
